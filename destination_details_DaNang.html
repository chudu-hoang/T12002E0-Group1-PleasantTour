Da Nang City

Description

Da Nang is an ideal destination. Danang city is green, clean and beautiful. In addition, Da Nang city owns beautiful beaches, smooth, white sand, voted by Forbes magazine as one of the 6 most attractive beaches on the planet. Da Nang city with poetic Han river and bridges have made the symbol of the coastal city. Da Nang also owns many beautiful landscapes that appeal to tourists such as Ba Na Hills, Son Tra Peninsula, Ngu Hanh Son...

In addition, Da Nang is the center of the Central Region, with a prime location located. On the Central Heritage road from Da Nang, you can come to 3 world cultural heritages: Hue Imperial Capital, Hoi An Ancient Town and My Son Sanctuary.

Day-01

Bus and tourguide will pick you up at hotel. Then delegation visit:
- Son Tra Peninsula: located about 10 km from the center of Da Nang. Linh Ung Pagoda has a Quan Am statue facing the East Sea, bringing peace to Danang, this is a famous spiritual spot of the Central.
- Ngu Hanh Son: These moutains located about 8 km from the center of the city, there are 5 mountains located along the Five Elements system, anyone who has ever come to Danang knows about Marble Mountains. The fanciful, dreamlike space, pagodas, caves, plants, temple bells, and the sound of the waves ... have created a natural paradise for visitors.
- Non Nuoc stone carving village, one of the four ancient villages in Quang Nam - Da Nang, where used to be focused on casting, carving and stone carving.
- Thuy Son Mountain: the most beautiful and largest mountain in Ngu Hanh Son.
- The works from Ha Thai, Trung Thai to Thuong Thai most famous such as Xa Loi Tower, Linh Ung Pagoda, Tang Chon Cave, Van Thong Cave, Vong Giang Dai, Tam Thai Pagoda... then tourists go down the mountain by road.

Have lunch at restaurant. Then tourguide take tourists to buy specialties at Specialty Supermarket in Da Nang. Bring tourists to the original pick-up point, end the tour program, farewell and see you again.
