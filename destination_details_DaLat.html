Da Lat

Description

A dreamy city, with the equally romantic beauty, Dalat tourists often call this place by many names: the city of thousands of flowers, the city of mist, the mountain town of Da Lat, or Little Paris, each name is filled with love.Tourism in Dalat is the destination and also one of the most interesting and special tourist destinations and a tourist destination for couples in love. is the paradise of relaxation and tourism is much loved.

Coming to Dalat tour, tourists can explore the vast pine forests, Dalat flower gardens all year round to show off their fragrance, Tuyen Lam Lake, Enjoy the clouds of Cau Dat tea hills, Xuan Xuan Lake, Dalat's Fresh garden, Me Linh Cafe, Ta Nung Flower Field, Hydrangea Flower Field, Thien Phuc Duc Hill, Stairway to Heaven - Suny farm, Bao Dai Palace, Zoodoo Zoo, Dalat Railway Station - all have created a poetic, lyrical Da Lat that makes people unable to go, people who go and remember forever only. Also invite you to refer to Dalat travel experience of Vietnam Tourism to introduce interesting places to eat and many useful information for you to refer before coming to this beautiful flower city.

Day-01

Bus and tourguide pick you up at the meeting point and depart to Dalat. Have breakfast at Nga Dau Dau cross. Then visit:
- Madagui tourist area, with a collection of bamboos of nearly 50 different types, discover the mysterious natural cave system, nature reserve with many wild animals living naturally.
- Exploring the thrilling game system such as: Kayaking, zipline, climbing, aerial walking, skiing, horse riding, paintball shooting... (expenses excluded)

Have lunch at Muong Xanh restaurant. Continue to visit:
- Truc Lam Zen Monastery, take the cable car through Robin hill (expenses excluded), admire the pine forest, Tuyen Lam lake, Phuong Hoang mountain from above.

Have dinner, check in and rest. Tourists are free to walk around the city of Da Lat at night, admire the scenery of Xuan Huong Lake, enjoy the taste of coffee in the mountain town. Overnight in Da Lat.

Day-02

Have breakfast. Then delegation visit:
- The team explored Hoa Son Dien Trang ecotourism site, the "Super Hot" destination recently, with the rustic and idyllic natural beauty of Japanese cherry blossom garden, Sapa ancient rose garden, flower fields triangle circuit... and especially the lucky hand.
- Me Linh coffee Garden, an open space designed. Tourists can both enjoy the mink coffee flavor (self-sufficient), and have a full view of Dalat.

Have lunch then visit:
- Vegetable and Flower Farm with an area of 50.000m2; visiting clean vegetable growing area cultivated by hydroponic method: lettuce, green lot, purple lot, Batavia green... and producing tubers, fruits: strawberries, black tomatoes, giant squash, melon pepino, woody tomatoes, purple kohlrabi and red radish; flower growing production area of more than 20 types such as roses, gerberas, Lavender, sunflowers, lilies, pistols, hydrangeas, ... shopping area for clean agricultural products and Dalat specialties.
- Coming to Dalat strawberry garden, tourists can pick for yourself and your loved each ripe berry, an indispensable gift when coming to the land of thousands of flowers.

In addition, tourists can attend campfire night, drink Can wine, gong performance, cultural exchanges with the Central Highlands (expenses excluded), stay overnight in Da Lat.

Day-03

Have breakfast, checkout. Touirsts are free to shop at Dalat market then delegation visit:
- Datanla tourist area, is the sights and adventure. Tourists can play slides - the only slide system in Dalat with the length of 1.000m winding around the slopes (cost is self-sufficient).
- Bus take delegation to Bao Loc for lunch and depart to Ho Chi Minh City. Ending the tour of Da Lat, farewell and see you again.
