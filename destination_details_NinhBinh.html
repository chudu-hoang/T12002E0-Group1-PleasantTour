Ninh Binh

Description

With pristine nature, charming landscapes, Ninh Binh is currently one of the famous destinations of the North. Coming to Ninh Binh, visitors will have the opportunity to visit the world natural and cultural heritages Trang An, Bai Dinh Pagoda, Tam Coc - Bich Dong, Van Long Nature Reserve or visit the ancient capital of Hoa. Lu, Phat Diem Stone Church.

Enjoy Video


Day-01

Tourists present at the domestic terminal, Tan Son Nhat airport at least three hours before flight. Representative of Plesant Tourist Company welcomes and supports you with procedures to catch the flight to Hanoi. At Noi Bai International Airport, the tour guide picks you up, then:
- Visiting Uncle Ho's Mausoleum (except Monday, Friday weekly maintenance of Mausoleum).
- Departure to Ha Long, to Yen Tu Mountain - a mountain of 1068 m above sea level, a natural landscape still preserving the system of cultural and historical relics associated with the birth, formation and development of Truc Lam Yen Tu Zen meditation, dubbed the "Vietnam Buddhist Land".

Have lunch, then delegation go up the mountain by cable car (self-supporting cable car), visit Hoa Yen Pagoda, Bao Thap, Truc Lam Tam To, Hang Tung 700 years old... go down the mountain to visit Truc Lam Zen Monastery with a Nhu Y sphere weighing 6 tons are ranked Vietnam guiness record. Depart to Ha Long.

Have dinner, overnight in Ha Long. Tourists are free to walk around, shop at the night market or join the Sunworld Ha Long Park with all indoor and outdoor games with the Dragon Park, Queen's Cable Car Sun wheel... ( expenses excluded).

Day-02

Have buffet breakfast at the hotel. Tourists take a boat to admire the Ha Long Bay - a world natural heritage with thousands of rocky islands in a strange shape - admire the elaborate beauty, the splendor of Thien Cung cave, the supernatural beauty of Dinh Huong island, Fighting Cock island, Rock Dog island...

Have lunch, depart to Trang An scenic spot in Ninh Binh, which is recognized by UNESCO as a mixed cultural and natural heritage of the world.

Have dinner, overnight in Ninh Binh. Tourists can walk freely, enjoy Ninh Binh specialties such as mountain goat meat, mountain snails, Yen Mac spring rolls...

Day-03

Have buffet breakfast at the hotel. Then:
- Go along a stream along the rice paddies in the middle of the rice field to visit Trang An tourist area where the limestone ranges, valleys and rivers intertwine to create a fanciful, mysterious space. Tourists explore Dark cave, Light cave, cave of wine making and learning about the history and culture here.

Have lunch, then delegation visit:
- Visit Bai Dinh pagoda - the temple with the most records in Vietnam (the temple with the largest area - the largest bronze Buddha statue in Vietnam).
- Bus pick delegation to Noi Bai airport for flight to Ho Chi Minh City. End the tour program, farewell and see you again.
