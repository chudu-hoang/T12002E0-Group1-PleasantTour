Phu Quoc beach

Description

Phu Quoc - known as "Pearl Island"- is really a great destination that you must book a tour right at our Pleasant Tour Office. Phu Quoc has a beautiful beaches that any tourist addict wants to check-in, it can be said that with the sea of Phu Quoc island, you can drop beautiful selfie station with every angle.
In addition, there is Vinpearl Safari resort zoo, built according to the world-famous safari model design with wild environment and space similar to the famous parks in Singapore or Thailand.
Tourists should not miss the country's famous specialties such as fish sauce, sim wine, skull pepper and precious pearl jewelry.

Enjoy Video


Day-01

Tourists present at the domestic terminal, Tan Son Nhat airport at least three hours before flight. Representative of Pleasant Tour Company welcomes and supports tourists with procedures to catch the flight to Phu Quoc.
- At Phu Quoc airport, the tour guide picks you up to visit the Pearl Culture Facility, learn about the cultivation process and you can choose the beauty products, jewelry made from pearls as gifts for relatives.
- Visiting Ho Quoc Pagoda (or Truc Lam Ho Quoc Zen Monastery), located on the ridge facing the sea at the end of a meandering path through the slopes of Phu Quoc Island, Truc Lam Ho Quoc Zen Monastery is one of the most beautiful pagodas in Vietnam.
- Depart to Bai Sao, you are free to swim at Bai Sao, the longest white sand beach and the most beautiful island, with clear blue water.
Have lunch then:
- Depart to the pier, tourists get on the boat to admire the sea, join the fishing program, swim and dive to see the colorful world of corals.
- Visit Sunset Sanato Beach Club, which is known as "The most beautiful sunset spot on Jade Island", located in the heart of Bai Truong. The sea space at Sunset Sanato Beach Club is very airy and is adorned with many unique architectural works, unforgettable. And perhaps it is these strange and creative architectural blocks that have helped Sunset Sanato Beach Club become a super check-in point.
Have dinner, overnight in Phu Quoc.

Day-02

Have breakfast.
Tourists can freely swim, relax at the hotel or you can choose the reference points of interest as follows:
Option 1: Explore VINWONDERS PHU QUOC'S PARK (Self-sufficient expenses; excluding transportation, entrance tickets to VinWonders + 2 lunches + dinners).
- VinWonders Phu Quoc - Park with 6 different themes, connected by the theme story throughout. Central castle area - Medieval Europe: Toursits will get lost in the center of a town full of medieval architecture with bustling shopping shops, with a unique combination of music, effects smoke, fire... - Viking Area - Secret Village: tourists will float on the river to discover the secrets hidden in Viking villages, where Urik prison and army have settled and buried treasures, or try to climb adventure climbing crept in the forest, the suspension bridge and zipline through the forest...
- Thrilling zone - adventure world: Discover the ancient world with games: Maya roller coaster, Firestorm coaster...
- Water park area - tornado world: Designed in the colors of Hawaii, with the first "giant" slides in Vietnam: snake venom slides, rage winds, raging pools giant waves...
- Fairy Tale - Magical World: Recreate the magical world of 5 continents through games designed with each story such as: Alice lost in wonderland, entering the Persian Oasis region mystery & guy Aladdin, Visit the Egyptian gods in the Ancient Valley...
- Aquarium - Hai Duong Palace: Tourists will visit the largest and most modern Aquarium in Vietnam, with the design of a giant sea turtle, carrying all the secrets of the ocean and the place of birth which has hundreds of fascinating marine creatures.


Option 2: Explore the Animal care & Conservation Vinpearl Safari Park (Self-sufficient expenses; excluding transportation, Safari tickets + 2 lunch + dinner)
- Semi-wildlife park: This is the first and only place in Vietnam to conserve all species in an open natural environment under the "safari" model. Without a separate barnyard system, more than 40 species of rare animals are grown and developed freely in the wild. Visitors will be moved through the Indian Tiger Conservation Area, African Rhino Conservation, Kenya Giraffe Conservation, Congo River Basin ... of semi-wildlife park by dedicated bus, bringing the most vivid and authentic experiences of the natural life of all species.
- Watch the animal show: The unique and endowed abilities of birds, mammals, reptiles (lemurs, snakes, parrots, hawks, etc.) will be displayed spectacularly in the Animal Performance program to bring visitors Interesting experiment. Notably, this program also helps visitors to better understand the behavior of species and interesting opportunities to interact directly with the lovely animal friends at Vinpearl Safari.
- Photographing with animals: keeping memorable moments with colorful, friendly animals such as royal pythons, Macaw parrots, green parrots, ferrets, weasel owl, baby crocodiles momentum, high sand, turtle.
- Feed the animals: tourists will take care of the gentle and friendly animals with their own hands. Giraffes, camels, elephants, gibbons, black and white foxes and birds are ready to welcome the special carers at Vinpearl Safari.
- In addition, there is a system of restaurants with diverse cuisine styles and shopping streets with unique souvenirs.

Day-03

Have breakfast then visit: - Dinh Cau landscape (Temple of the Dragon King) such as the symbol of Phu Quoc where the sea, sand, sun and rocks mix together into a charming scenery. This place is not only a beautiful sight, with interesting shapes but also a sacred and ancient land.
- The beautiful landscapes and produce in Phu Quoc such as pepper garden, sim wine production base, anchovy fish sauce production tank.
Have lunch then: - Visiting Ham Ninh fishing village, tourists can enjoy Ham Ninh specialties or buy as gifts for your loved ones: crab, walrus live in wine, crispy walrus, pineapple in the sea and raven beak to soak medicinal wine... These are specialties only available in Phu Quoc (self-sufficient expenses).
- Tour guide escort the delegation to Phu Quoc airport, flight to Ho Chi Minh City. Finish the tour, farewell and see you again.
